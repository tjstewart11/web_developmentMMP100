The Casino

Ted decides he's feeling lucky and goes to the casino. It must be a lucky day, as the casino is crowded and the only game available is blackjack. Not wanting to just go back home, he sits at a table as cards are dealt. The dealer goes over the rules, stating that the goal is to get 21 or as close as possible without going over. stands, pretty sure the next card would put him over 21
